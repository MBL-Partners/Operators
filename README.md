# AI Readiness Diagnostic

A structured 10-minute diagnostic that scores your organization's AI readiness across six dimensions and tells you exactly where to focus before committing budget to an AI initiative.

Built by [MBL Partners](https://mblpartners.com) — part of the Thrive with AI skill library.

---

## What It Does

Runs you through six scored questions across the dimensions that most often decide whether an AI initiative sticks or stalls:

1. **Security** — How is your data protected when your team uses AI tools?
2. **Integration** — How connected is AI across your workflows and platforms?
3. **Quality & Governance** — Who owns the quality of AI-generated work?
4. **Measurement** — How do you measure what AI is actually worth to the business?
5. **Institutional Memory** — Does your AI get smarter over time, or does every session start from scratch?
6. **Adoption & Scaling** — How do you spread AI use beyond the people who figured it out on their own?

Plus two context questions about your productivity suite and IT infrastructure to tailor the output.

After all answers are collected, it scores your responses (6–24) and produces a readiness snapshot: what's working, where the gaps are, and 1–3 specific actions to take in the next 30 days.

---

## Score Tiers

| Score | Stage | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 6–11 | Foundation | Ad hoc at best. Basics need to be in place before any initiative will stick. |
| 12–17 | Building | Some foundations exist, but gaps are wide enough to break a new initiative. |
| 18–21 | Advancing | Solid in places. Ready to move, but specific gaps will create friction. |
| 22–24 | Compounding | New AI initiatives can compound on what's already working. Focus on scale. |

## Install

1. Download `ai-readiness-diagnostic.skill`
2. Open Cowork
3. Drag the `.skill` file into your Cowork window
4. The skill installs automatically and is ready to run

---

## How to Run It

Once installed, trigger it by saying any of the following:

- *"Run the readiness diagnostic"*
- *"How ready is my team for AI?"*
- *"AI maturity assessment"*
- *"Should we start an AI initiative?"*

The skill asks one question at a time and waits for your response before moving on. It takes about ten minutes.

---

## Output

At the end you receive a **Readiness Snapshot** with:

- Your score and tier
- The 1–2 dimensions where you're strongest
- The 1–2 gaps most likely to break an initiative
- Specific, 30-day actions sized to your actual situation
- A context note if you're on Microsoft 365 or using a managed IT provider
